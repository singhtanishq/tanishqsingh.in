D. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze usage, and deliver personalized content.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as page navigation, session management, and security features. These cookies do not require consent.

Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how you use our website, such as pages visited, time spent, and errors encountered. This data helps us improve website performance and user experience. Examples include Google Analytics cookies.

Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences and settings, such as language choice, theme preference, or login information, to provide a personalized experience on subsequent visits.

Marketing Cookies

These cookies track your interactions with our website and may be shared with third-party advertising networks to deliver targeted advertisements. These require your explicit consent.

Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. However, blocking essential cookies may impair website functionality. For more information on managing cookies, visit allaboutcookies.org.

Third-Party Tracking

We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics to track and analyze website usage. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

G. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

GDPR Rights (for EEA residents)

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your data under certain circumstances (the "right to be forgotten").
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Right to Object: You can object to processing based on legitimate interests or for marketing purposes.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: You can file a complaint with your local data protection authority.

CCPA Rights (for California residents)

  • Right to Know: You can request what personal information we collect, use, and share.
  • Right to Delete: You can request deletion of personal information we have collected.
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information.
  • Right to Correct: You can request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.

DPDPA Rights (for Indian residents)

  • Right to Access: You can request access to your personal data.
  • Right to Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your data.
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: You can file a grievance with our Data Protection Officer.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in Section J (Contact Us). We will respond to your request within the timeframe specified by applicable law (typically 30 days for GDPR, 45 days for CCPA, and 30 days for DPDPA).
